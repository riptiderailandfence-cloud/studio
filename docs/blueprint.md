# **App Name**: PillarPath

## Core Features:

- Tenant & User Management: Secure multi-tenant architecture with Firebase Authentication for business users and strict Firestore security rules to prevent cross-tenant data access.
- CRM & Lead Management: Comprehensive tools for managing customer and lead information, including contact details, addresses, notes, and tracking pipeline stages.
- Materials & Style Builder: Interface to define and manage materials with costs, then construct custom fence, post, and gate styles with integrated Bills of Material and real-time cost calculation.
- Advanced Estimate Generation: Generate detailed project estimates using custom styles, apply flexible pricing strategies (margin/markup), save cost snapshots, and manage estimate status workflows.
- Client Portal & Magic Deposits: A secure, token-accessed client portal for customers to view estimates, receive status updates, and make deposit payments via Magic.
- Crew & Job Assignment: Functionality to manage crew members, define their various rates (hourly, labor, production), and assign them to specific jobs linked to accepted estimates.
- AI-Powered Estimate Optimization: A tool that analyzes historical project data within the tenant's account to suggest competitive pricing strategies or cost-effective material combinations for new estimates.

## Style Guidelines:

- A light color scheme to convey clarity and professionalism.
- Primary color: A deep, professional blue (#336699) reflecting stability and trust.
- Background color: A very light, subtle grey-blue (#F1F5F9) for a clean and unobtrusive canvas.
- Accent color: A vibrant blue-purple (#3B3BDC) to highlight important actions and call-to-action elements with strong contrast.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, objective aesthetic and excellent readability across various content types.
- Clean, professional, and intuitive outline-style icons to ensure clear communication and ease of navigation throughout the application.
- A structured, responsive layout featuring clear module-based sections and a consistent sidebar navigation for efficient user workflows across devices.
- Subtle and purposeful transitions for user interactions such as form submissions and navigation changes, providing smooth feedback without being distracting.
